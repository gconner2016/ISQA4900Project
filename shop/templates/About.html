  About Us

 Robo Depot was formed in 2022 by its three founders, Blake Kinnaman, Salim Al Maqbali, and Aaron Strong. Though our team is small,
 the three of us are dedicated to bringing our users an exceptional variety of top-of-the-line robot products. Robo Depo is on a
 mission to provide customers with friendly, task-oriented robots that are designed to make their owner's life a little easier.
 Whether you need assistance cleaning around the house, a tutor for a specific subject, or a small pet companion, Robo Depot is
 happy to supply customers a multitude of possibilities.



 Robo Depo's base of operations is located in Omaha, NE, which is coincidentally where the three of us met. After attending the
 University of Nebraska at Omaha together, we all decided to go in on a business venture, and after some initial failed business ideas,
 we found that we all shared a passion for the robotic arts. After securing some blueprints and patents for our various products,
 found that the best way to operate would be through an online storefront, as it provides us with a wide audience across the internet
 along with giving us the freedom to work anywhere at any time. Despite being a primarily online based business, we have future plans to
 open multiple storefronts across the country, and maybe come to a town near you!
