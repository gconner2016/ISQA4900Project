  Frequently Asked Questions

      Q: How long is the typical wait time for a delivery?

      A: It varies by product, but a typical shipment will take 3 to 5 business days.
     The shipping dates also vary depending on where federal holidays land within an order timeframe.


      Q: What measures are put in place to prevent my robot from being stolen?

      A: To activate a robot, we require users to input a confirmation ID code that we send them once their delivery has arrived.
     This ID code expires within 24 hours, but a new code is sent out if the user emails us to send another.


      Q: What is Robo Depo's return policy?

      A: For our return policy, we will allow returns for robots that have been purchased and activated within a one-month time frame,
     and if the robot is in an acceptable functioning condition.


      Q: What can I do if I never receive my order?

      A: In this circumstance, users will need to contact us directly so we can confirm that their robot is missing.
     Once we confirm the issue, we will activate a built-in tracking system to find the robot and send it back to its owner.


      Q: What can I do if my robot arrives damaged?

      A: In this circumstance, users will need to contact us directly so we can send out a damages form.
     The form will ask for the robot's identification information and will also ask for pictures of the damages.


      Q: Is there a warranty for the robots.

      A: Yes, we offer an optional 2-year warranty for all our robot products.
     The warranty covers any malfunctions, electrical failures, mechanical failures, and manufacturing defects that cause the robot to not fulfil its intended functions.


      Q: Can I make changes to an order I already placed.

      A: Users can make changes to an order up to 48 hours after it was placed.
     Depending on when new additions are made, users may need to wait an additional business day for their order to arrive.


      Q: What happens if the Robo Depo website crashes/shuts down while I am in the middle of an order?

      A: We will let users know in advance the time frame of scheduled website maintenance
     and will have a pop-up that warn activate users to quickly finish their order before the website closes.


      Q: Will there be any customization options for the robots you have?

      A: We plan to add multiple customization options to all our robots soon.
     These features would include changing the color of the robot, as well as adding attachments to some of our higher end robots.
